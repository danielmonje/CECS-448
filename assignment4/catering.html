Truck Highlights

  • Perfect for events of all sizes
  • Completely self-sufficient
  • Truck can be booked for a food minimum of $1,700 plus tax.
  • Charburger with cheese, French fries, and a beverage start at $11.45 per person, plus tax.
  • The Food minimum feeds up to 148 guests.
  • Upon booking we require a $500 deposit signed contract. The deposit will be applied to the overall cost of the event.
  • Standard Service time is 1.5 hours and $175 for each additional 1/2 hour.
  • Our Truck can accommodate 275 guests per hour (Charburger w/Cheese & Veggie Burger Combo Meals Only, without customizations).
  • Suggested menu for 200 or more guests: Single Charburger w/Cheese & Veggie Burger Combo Meals.
  • Habit Trucks will travel 1 hour and 60 miles roundtrip. Each additional 30 minutes of drive time is $150 and $2.75 per mile.
  • The Truck will arrive 45 minutes before the scheduled service time to set up.
  • Final payment is due 10 days before the event date.
  • A $300 late night fee will apply to events scheduled between the hours of 9:00 pm – 11:59 pm.
  • A $500 late night fee will apply to events scheduled between the hours of 12:00 am – 7:00 am.
  • A $300 holiday fee will be applied to the following Holidays: New Year’s Day, Memorial Day, 4th of July, Labor Day, Halloween, Christmas Eve, New Year’s Eve.
  • The Habit Truck will provide napkins, condiments, and trash cans. Tables and chairs for guests are not provided.
  • The Habit Truck can provide business license permits, health department permits and certificates of insurance. Other permits, such as park permits, and entrance fees will need to be secured by the guest.
  • A map and directions of how to get to your location and where the Truck will be parked is appreciated.
  • Prices, terms, and conditions are subject to change.
