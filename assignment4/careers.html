The Habit Difference

The Habit difference is our people. The foundation of our culture is genuine respect. Great customer service is a result of a well-trained team, taught to respect their job, the guests, and each other. We will show your career development the same respect we show all aspects of the “HABIT DIFFERENCE”. You may join our team as a cook, cashier, or enter management with prior experience. Our training stresses the “why” behind everything you learn creating leaders, not followers.

Q: ARE YOU HIRING?

We are always looking for friendly and motivated Cooks, Cashiers and Managers to join Our Team. Click here to learn more and apply!

Q: I DON’T HAVE A RESUME, HOW DO I APPLY?

Please visit us online at www.habitburger.com/careers to complete an application. You may also apply in person at any of our locations.

Q: DO I NEED EXPERIENCE?

Entry-level positions only require the right attitude, availability and motivation. You can also include any previous work, school or volunteer experience.
Experienced cooks and cashier applicants may add their work history to the application.
We recommend manager applications include a formal resume with all relevant work history information.

Q: WHAT ARE THE REQUIREMENTS TO WORK AT THE HABIT?

  • 1 year experience in food service/hospitality desired but not required
  • Customer service focused and solution oriented
  • Must enjoy and be able to succeed in a fast-paced work environment
  • Ability to communicate clearly and professionally with customers and coworkers
  • Basic math and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to work a flexible schedule based on Restaurant needs
  • Food handler’s card may be required according to local and or state regulations. California Applicants: A California Food Handler Card is required.
Shared “Habit” values:
  • H – Hospitality: satisfy & delight our customers
  • A – Attitude: enthusiastic, positive energy
  • B – Be the best: daily pursuit of excellence
  • I – Integrity: do the right thing, even when difficult
  • T – Teamwork: do your job well and help others do theirs
